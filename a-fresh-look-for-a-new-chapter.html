A Fresh Look for a New Chapter

Welcome, fellow creatives, developers, and visitors alike! Today marks an exciting chapter in the journey of my digital presence. After three years of showcasing my work with the foundational elements of Projects, Resume, and Request CV, I am thrilled to unveil the new face of my portfolio website.

Even if I knew that tomorrow the world would go to pieces, I would still plant my apple tree.

Martin Luther

Embracing Change

Change is inevitable, and as creators, we must embrace it. Over the past few months, I've delved deep into introspection, reflecting on how far I've come and where I envision myself heading next. This process of self-discovery has led to the realization that my online identity needs to evolve alongside my personal and professional growth.

Vision

With a clear vision in mind, I embarked on the journey of reimagining my digital presence. I wanted my portfolio to not only reflect my skills and accomplishments but also to convey my personality, values, and aspirations. Every design element, color choice, and layout decision was made with intention, aiming to create a cohesive and immersive experience for my visitors.

The New Look

At the heart of the revamped portfolio lies a carefully curated visual identity that aims to captivate and engage. Drawing inspiration from contemporary design trends and personal preferences, I've embraced a fresh color palette dominated by the vibrant hue of #0075F6. This striking shade of blue not only exudes confidence and professionalism but also adds a touch of playfulness to the overall aesthetic.

primary = #0075F6
dark = #040405
light = #FBFBFD
header = IBM Plex Sans
body = Inter

Complementing the bold color choice are the meticulously selected typefaces: IBM Plex Sans for headers and Inter for body text, paragraphs, lists, and more. The combination of these fonts strikes a perfect balance between sophistication and readability, enhancing the overall user experience.

Moreover, the layout has undergone a significant transformation to ensure a seamless and intuitive browsing experience. Each element is thoughtfully placed to optimize visual hierarchy and content flow, guiding visitors on a journey of exploration and discovery. From the streamlined navigation to the dynamic animations, every aspect of the layout has been fine-tuned to delight and inspire.
